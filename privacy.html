Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Figure does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

Figure is a daily earnings tracker for self-employed creatives. We built it with privacy as a foundation — not an afterthought. Your financial data is personal, and it belongs to you.

Data We Collect

We collect nothing. Figure does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, financial data, or usage data to any server or third party.

All data you enter into Figure — earnings, expenses, pay periods, settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Figure does not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking frameworks, or cloud services. There are no third parties involved in the operation of this app.

iCloud

Figure does not currently use iCloud sync. Your data exists only on the device where you installed the app.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Figure uses Apple's local notification system. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

Figure does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is nothing to protect.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy — for example, if we add optional cloud features in a future version — we will update this page and note the date of change. We will never introduce data collection without being transparent about it.

Your Rights

Since Figure stores all data locally on your device, you are in complete control. You can delete the app at any time to remove all associated data from your device.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at hello@tryfigure.app
